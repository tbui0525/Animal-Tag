The Crewmate possesses three distinct abilities. Prior to the activation of any specific ability, a mandatory "No ability" phase must be executed. When the Crewmate is unequipped with headgear, the player shall not have access to any ability.
- D1-1. Toilet Paper: When the Crewmate has Toilet Paper on its head, Only the backside of the player in possession may be targeted.
- D1-2. Leaf: When the Crewmate has a Leaf on its head, the player will be immune to carnivorous sabotages (eg. Dog) and get to double use herbivorous/omnivorous self-helps (eg. Turtle, Lion Unicorn) without needing an intermediary creature possession.
- D1-3. Scrambled Eggs: When the Crewmate has an Egg on its head, everyone is forced to swap creature possessions. Whoever is closest to “It” becomes “It.”
